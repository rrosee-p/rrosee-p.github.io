Pacific Rhododendron

A common name for the Pacific Rhododendron are Coast Rhododendron, California Rhododendron, and California RoseBay. The height of the plant ranges from 2 to 9 meters and the width can spread to 3-5 meters. The range of geography can be from Oregon, Washington State, British Columbia, and California. The best climate for this plant can be found in cool and moist climate or moderate humidity.

Spanish Rice

A flavorful, simple Spanish rice recipe your whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups washed rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • Oil (for browning rice)
  • Seasonings:
    • Onion powder
    • Garlic powder
    • Salt & pepper
    • Chicken bouillon powder

Directions

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add oil and allow it to warm up.
  3. Add rice and stir until lightly browned.
  4. Mix in tomato paste until well combined.
  5. Pour in 3 cups of water.
  6. Stir in 2 tablespoons of each seasoning.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 30 minutes.
  8. Turn off heat and let rest for 15 minutes before serving.

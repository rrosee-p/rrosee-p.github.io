Spanish Rice

A delicious and easy to make recipe. Use washed rice for a better taste!

Ingredients

  • Spanish Rice Ingredients
    • 2 cups of washed rice
    • 3 cups of water
    • Tomato Paste
  • Seasonings
    • Onion Seasoning
    • Garlic Seasoning
    • Salt & Pepper Seasoning
    • Chicken Poullion Seasoning

Directions

  • Preheat pan to a medium heat
  • Add oil and let it heat up
  • Add rice and stir until golden brown
  • Then add tomato paste
  • Mix around for 2 minutes then add 3 cups of water
  • Mix again and add 2 tablespoons of all seasonings
  • Mix until fully dispursed throughout rice
  • Turn down the heat and put top cover over pan and let sit for 30 minutes
  • After 30 minutes turn off heat and let sit for 15 minutes

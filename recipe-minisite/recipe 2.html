French Toast

A delicious and easy to make recipe!

Ingredients

  • Spanish Rice Ingredients
    • 6 slices of white bread (or whichever bread you prefer)
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 cup of whole milk
    • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
    • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
    • 3 tbsp butter
    • Whipped Cream
    • Sliced Strawberries
    • Maple Syrup

Directions

  • In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, nutmeg and cinnamon.
  • Coat each slice of prferred bread evenly in egg mixture.
  • Bring a skillet to medium heat and add 1 tbsp butter.
  • Add 2 slices of bread and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, until golden brown. Repeat for remaining slices.
  • Add 3 slices to each plate and top with sliced strawberries, a dallop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of powdered sugar.
  • Serve with maple syrup
  • And enjoy!

French Toast

A delicious and easy-to-make breakfast favorite.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bread (your choice)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • Whipped cream, strawberries, maple syrup (optional)

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, nutmeg, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  2. Dip each bread slice into the mixture to coat.
  3. Heat a skillet to medium and add 1 tbsp butter.
  4. Cook 2 slices at a time ~2–3 minutes per side until golden.
  5. Serve with strawberries, whipped cream, and syrup.

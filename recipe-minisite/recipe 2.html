This French toast recipe is simple, comforting, and perfect for starting your day with something sweet.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of white bread (or preferred bread)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • Whipped cream
  • Sliced strawberries
  • Maple syrup

Directions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, nutmeg, and cinnamon.
  2. Coat each slice of bread evenly in the egg mixture.
  3. Heat a skillet to medium heat and add 1 tbsp butter.
  4. Cook 2 slices of bread for 2â€“3 minutes per side until golden. Repeat for remaining slices.
  5. Plate 3 slices per serving, top with strawberries, whipped cream, and powdered sugar.
  6. Serve with maple syrup.
  7. Enjoy!

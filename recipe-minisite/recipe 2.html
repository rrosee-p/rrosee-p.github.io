Classic French Toast

Start your morning right with golden, buttery French toast — crisp on the edges, soft in the center, and dusted with a touch of cinnamon and powdered sugar. Perfect for a cozy breakfast or brunch!

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 8 slices of thick bread (brioche or challah recommended)
  • Butter, for frying
  • Powdered sugar and maple syrup, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a shallow bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon until well combined.
  2. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat and add a pat of butter.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, coating both sides evenly.
  4. Place the soaked bread onto the skillet and cook until golden brown on each side, about 2–3 minutes per side.
  5. Serve warm with butter, powdered sugar, and maple syrup.

Tips

For the best results, use slightly stale bread — it soaks up the custard mixture without getting soggy. Add a pinch of nutmeg for extra warmth, or top with fresh berries for a fruity twist!

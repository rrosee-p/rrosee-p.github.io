French Toast

Warm, fluffy, cinnamon-kissed slices — weekend vibes.

Quick facts

Prep: 8 min · Cook: 10 min · Serves 2–3

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • Whipped cream & berries to serve

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, nutmeg, and cinnamon in a wide bowl.
  2. Dip bread slices to coat both sides.
  3. Heat skillet with butter and cook slices 2–3 minutes per side until golden.
  4. Serve topped with berries, whipped cream, and maple syrup.

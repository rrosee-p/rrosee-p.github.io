French Toast

A quick and easy breakfast treat!

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • Whipped cream
  • Sliced strawberries
  • Maple syrup

Directions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, nutmeg, and cinnamon in a bowl.
  2. Dip bread slices in egg mixture evenly.
  3. Heat skillet on medium heat and melt butter.
  4. Cook 2 slices at a time for 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Plate, top with strawberries, whipped cream, and powdered sugar.
  6. Serve with maple syrup and enjoy!

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • 4 oz pancetta
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crisp.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan together.
  4. Combine everything off-heat and season.

Homemade Brownies

A delicious and easy to make recipe.

Ingredients

  • Ingredients
    • Baking Spray
    • 2 cups of white sugar
    • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup of butter
    • 4 large eggs
    • 1/2 cocoa powder
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1/2 tsp baking powder
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/2 walnuts

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x13-inch pan with baking spray.
  • Whisk sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  • Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  • Add walnuts.
  • Bake for 20-30 minutes until crinkled on top and insert toothpick to test moisture insead.
  • Let cool and enjoy!

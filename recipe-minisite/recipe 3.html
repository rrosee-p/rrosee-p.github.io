Homemade Brownies

Fudgy brownies with optional walnuts — simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Baking spray
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup walnuts (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, butter, eggs, cocoa, vanilla, baking powder, and salt until combined.
  3. Spread batter into the pan and sprinkle walnuts on top.
  4. Bake 20–30 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool before cutting.

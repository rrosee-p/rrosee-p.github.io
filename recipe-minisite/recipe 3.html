Homemade Brownies

Rich, chocolatey, and perfect for dessert!

Ingredients

  • Baking spray
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 cup melted butter
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup walnuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease 9x13 pan.
  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa, vanilla, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Spread batter in pan and sprinkle walnuts on top.
  4. Bake 20-30 minutes, test with toothpick.
  5. Cool before cutting. Enjoy!

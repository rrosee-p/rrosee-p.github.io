Matcha Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp matcha powder
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 prepared graham cracker crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Beat cream cheese, sugar, and matcha until smooth.
  3. Add heavy cream and blend until fluffy.
  4. Pour mixture into crust, bake 25–30 minutes.
  5. Cool and chill before serving.

Homemade Brownies

A delicious and easy-to-make recipe!

Ingredients

  • Baking spray
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup walnuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch pan with baking spray.
  2. Whisk sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  3. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Sprinkle walnuts on top.
  5. Bake for 20–30 minutes until the top is crinkled and a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Allow to cool before cutting. Enjoy!

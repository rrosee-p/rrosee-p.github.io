Homemade Brownies

Rich, fudgy, and absolutely craveable.

Quick facts

Prep: 10 min · Cook: 25 min · Serves 9–12

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup walnuts (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a 9×13 pan.
  2. Whisk sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa, vanilla, baking powder, and salt until combined.
  3. Pour batter into the pan and sprinkle walnuts on top if using.
  4. Bake 20–30 minutes until a toothpick comes out mostly clean.
  5. Cool completely before slicing for best results.

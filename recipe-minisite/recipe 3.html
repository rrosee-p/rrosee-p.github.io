Classic French Toast

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 8 slices brioche bread
  • Butter for frying
  • Maple syrup and powdered sugar

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  2. Dip bread slices into the mixture.
  3. Cook on a buttered skillet until golden brown on each side.
  4. Serve with syrup and sugar.

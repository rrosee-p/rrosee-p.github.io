Spanish Rice

A simple and tasty recipe using washed rice!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups washed rice
  • 3 cups water
  • Tomato paste

Seasonings

  • Onion seasoning
  • Garlic seasoning
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Chicken bouillon

Directions

  1. Preheat pan to medium heat.
  2. Add oil and heat up.
  3. Add rice, stir until golden brown.
  4. Add tomato paste and mix for 2 minutes.
  5. Add water and seasonings.
  6. Stir until combined.
  7. Cover pan, cook 30 minutes on low heat.
  8. Turn off heat, let sit 15 minutes.

Spanish Rice

A delicious and easy to make recipe. Use washed rice for a better taste!

Ingredients

  • Spanish Rice Ingredients
    • 2 cups of washed rice
    • 3 cups of water
    • Tomato Paste
  • Seasonings
    • Onion Seasoning
    • Garlic Seasoning
    • Salt & Pepper Seasoning
    • Chicken Poullion Seasoning

Directions

  1. Preheat pan to a medium heat
  2. Add oil and let it heat up
  3. Add rice and stir until golden brown
  4. Then add tomato paste
  5. Mix around for 2 minutes then add 3 cups of water
  6. Mix again and add 2 tablespoons of all seasonings
  7. Mix until fully dispursed throughout rice
  8. Turn down the heat and put top cover over pan and let sit for 30 minutes
  9. After 30 minutes turn off heat and let sit for 15 minutes

Spanish Rice

A delicious and easy-to-make recipe. Use washed rice for a better taste!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of washed rice
  • 3 cups of water
  • Tomato paste

Seasonings

  • Onion seasoning
  • Garlic seasoning
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Chicken bouillon

Directions

  1. Preheat pan to medium heat.
  2. Add oil and let it heat up.
  3. Add rice and stir until golden brown.
  4. Add tomato paste.
  5. Mix for 2 minutes, then add 3 cups of water.
  6. Add 2 tablespoons of each seasoning.
  7. Stir until well combined.
  8. Reduce heat, cover pan, and cook for 30 minutes.
  9. Turn off heat and let sit for 15 minutes.

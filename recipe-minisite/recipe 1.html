Spanish Rice

A delicious and easy to make recipe. Use washed rice for best results.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups washed rice
  • 3 cups water
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion seasoning
  • Garlic seasoning
  • Salt & pepper
  • Chicken bouillon

Directions

  1. Preheat pan to medium heat.
  2. Add oil and warm it up.
  3. Add rice and stir until golden brown.
  4. Stir in tomato paste and cook ~2 minutes.
  5. Add 3 cups water and 2 tbsp seasoning, stir to combine.
  6. Lower heat, cover, and simmer 30 minutes.
  7. Turn off heat and let rice rest 15 minutes before serving.

Spanish Rice

A warm, comfort classic — simple, flavorful, and satisfying.

✨ A Family Favorite Recipe

This Spanish rice is quick, easy, and absolutely delicious. Perfect for weeknight dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of washed rice
  • 3 cups of water
  • Tomato paste

Seasonings

  • Onion seasoning
  • Garlic seasoning
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Chicken bouillon

Directions

  1. Preheat pan to medium heat.
  2. Add oil and let it heat up.
  3. Add rice and stir until golden brown.
  4. Add tomato paste.
  5. Mix for 2 minutes, then add 3 cups of water.
  6. Add 2 tablespoons of each seasoning.
  7. Stir until well blended.
  8. Reduce heat, cover, and cook for 30 minutes.
  9. Turn off heat and let rest for 15 minutes.

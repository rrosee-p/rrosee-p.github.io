Spanish Rice

A warm family favorite — toasty rice with tomato and seasonings.

Quick facts

Prep: 10 min · Cook: 35 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups washed rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste

Seasonings

  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon powder

Directions

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a splash of oil.
  2. Add rice and stir until lightly golden.
  3. Stir in tomato paste until evenly coated.
  4. Pour in 3 cups water and add seasonings.
  5. Bring to a simmer, reduce to low, cover, and cook 30 minutes.
  6. Turn off heat and let sit 10–15 minutes before fluffing and serving.

Little Red Riding Hood

A story of a young girl, a wolf, and an important lesson.

Scroll ↓

On Her Way

Little Red Riding Hood sets off to visit her grandmother, carrying a basket of treats through the deep forest.

The Wolf Appears

A cunning wolf meets her along the path, asking where she’s going. He hatches a plan to reach her grandmother first.

At Grandmother’s House

The wolf arrives at the house, tricks Grandmother, and waits in disguise for Little Red Riding Hood.

Rescue

A passing woodcutter hears the commotion, enters the house, and saves Little Red Riding Hood and her grandmother.

Story by: Traditional Fairy Tale

The Tortoise and the Hare

A classic fable about patience, perseverance, and humility.

The Hare Brags

The speedy hare laughed at the slow tortoise, claiming he would easily win any race.

Off They Go!

The race started, and the hare sprinted ahead while the tortoise moved steadily forward.

Overconfidence

The hare grew confident and took a nap halfway through the race, thinking he had plenty of time.

Slow and Steady Wins

The tortoise continued patiently and crossed the finish line first, teaching the hare a valuable lesson.

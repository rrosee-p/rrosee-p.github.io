Little Red Riding Hood

A story of a young girl, a wolf, and an important lesson.

The Journey

Little Red Riding Hood walks through the forest carrying a basket for her grandmother.

The Wolf Appears

A sly wolf meets her on the path and asks where she is going.

Grandmother in Danger

The wolf reaches the house before her, tricks Grandmother, and waits in disguise for Little Red Riding Hood.

Rescue

A passing woodcutter hears the commotion and saves Little Red Riding Hood and her grandmother.

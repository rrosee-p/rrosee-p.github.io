The Lost Dog

One sunny afternoon, a small dog named Milo wandered away from home.

The Forest

Milo followed a butterfly into the woods, not realizing how far he had gone.

The River

He reached a river and barked for help, hoping someone would hear him.

The Search

Back home, his family searched everywhere, calling his name.

Reunited

A friendly hiker heard Milo and returned him safely to his family.

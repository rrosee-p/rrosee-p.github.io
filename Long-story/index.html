Little Red Riding Hood

A story of a young girl, a wolf, and a lesson in listening carefully.

Scroll ↓

The Journey

Little Red Riding Hood walks through the forest carrying a basket for her grandmother. She is warned not to stray from the path.

The Wolf Appears

The wolf meets her and asks where she’s going. He hatches a plan to get to her grandmother’s house first.

Grandmother in Danger

The wolf reaches the house, tricks the grandmother, and waits in disguise for Little Red Riding Hood to arrive.

Rescue

A passing woodcutter hears the commotion and saves Little Red Riding Hood and her grandmother. The lesson is learned: always listen carefully.

Story by: Traditional Fairy Tale

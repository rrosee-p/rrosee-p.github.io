About Me

Learn more about who I am and my journey as a photographer.

Hi, I'm Rose Pineda

I’ve been passionate about photography for many years, capturing moments that tell a story and preserving memories for my clients. My style blends creativity, authenticity, and fun—so every session feels natural and enjoyable.

Over the years, I’ve worked with families, couples, and individuals to create photos that truly reflect their personality and the joy in their lives. Whether it’s a milestone, a celebration, or just a special day, I strive to make every session memorable.

When you book with me, you’re not just getting photos—you’re getting an experience. I believe in creating a comfortable environment where you can be yourself and have fun while I capture these moments.

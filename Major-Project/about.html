About Me

Hi! My name is Rose Pineda, and I’ve been passionate about photography since I was 14 years old. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills and focused on portrait photography, capturing intimate and authentic moments that tell a story.

I specialize in graduation, engagement, couples, and personal portrait sessions. I believe that every person deserves beautiful, timeless photos that reflect who they are. My goal is to create a relaxed, comfortable environment so that your true personality shines through in every shot.

Though I only photograph portraits, my dedication to detail and creativity ensures each session produces high-quality images you’ll cherish forever.
